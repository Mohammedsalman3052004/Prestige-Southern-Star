Overview Prestige Southern Star At Begur Road, Bangalore

Prestige Southern Star is an iconic residential masterpiece by Prestige Group, perfectly positioned on Begur Road, Akshayanagar, opposite DLF Western Heights, off Bannerghatta Road, Bengaluru. Spanning an impressive 34 acres, this landmark development blends architectural brilliance with a lifestyle of unmatched luxury.

Comprising 14 gracefully designed towers and 2,130 premium residences, the project offers thoughtfully curated 3, 3.5, and 4 BHK apartments. Each tower rises 26 or 27 floors above a double-basement and ground level, offering expansive interiors, abundant natural light, and sweeping panoramic views of the city skyline.

Every detail is crafted for sophistication from lush landscaped gardens and a world-class clubhouse to a resort-style swimming pool, state-of-the-art fitness centre, and 24/7 security.

Prestige Southern Star isnt just a home its a lifestyle destination. Its prime location ensures excellent connectivity to major IT corridors, top educational institutions, reputed healthcare facilities, and vibrant entertainment hubs, making it an exceptional choice for discerning homebuyers and astute investors alike.

About Prestige Group

Prestige Group stands as one of Indias most trusted and admired real estate developers, celebrated for redefining skylines and creating landmark destinations across the country. Since its inception in 1986, the Group has been a driving force in shaping urban landscapes in key cities such as Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, and Kochi.

With a rich legacy spanning over three decades, Prestige has delivered an impressive portfolio of projects from luxury residences and integrated townships to IT parks, retail hubs, malls, and hospitality landmarks. Each development reflects the brands hallmark values of superior construction quality, innovative design, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Prestiges iconic creations including Prestige Shantiniketan, Prestige Falcon City, Prestige Lakeside Habitat, and the renowned Forum Malls have become synonymous with style, functionality, and world-class living.

Guided by a strong focus on sustainability, customer satisfaction, and timely delivery, Prestige Group continues to set industry benchmarks, turning ambitious visions into enduring realities.
